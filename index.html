FAQ

What is MOVE experience ?

The MOVE experience includes access to a weekly curriculum of live and on-demand dance classes in all styles and levels with the world’s top choreographers. Our classes include choreography, technique, fundamentals, strength and conditioning, career advice, and so much more! Each week will culminate with an Interactive Zoom Class with live performances. You’ll also get the chance to interact live with instructors and get real-time feedback.

How do I access the content ?

Upon registration, you’ll be automatically enrolled in the MOVE experience. The MOVE experience can be found directly through your favourite social media pages for MOVE. If you’re already a member, you can log into your account and enroll via the experience banner at the top of the homepage.

What age and level are ideal for the courses ?

This programs are designed for kids ages 8+, teens, and adults. We offer beginner, intermediate, and advanced tracks, but recommend having at least some dance experience prior to participating.

How do I cancel ?

If you’d like to cancel your MOVE membership, you can cancel your renewal at any time by writing to us. You will continue to have access to all of our content until your subscription’s expiration date.
